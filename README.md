## <a name="colors"></a> **1. Colors**

NEW's colors are black-white shade. Color system has separated into black, white, and shades of grey.


![https://raw.githubusercontent.com/THE-EXISTING/NEW/main/Assets/colors.png](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/THE-EXISTING/NEW/main/Assets/colors.png)

Available colors:

- Black
- White
- Grey10 (Light at 10% on HSL)
- Grey20 (Light at 20% on HSL)
- Grey30 (Light at 30% on HSL)
- Grey40 (Light at 40% on HSL)
- Grey50 (Light at 50% on HSL)
- Grey60 (Light at 60% on HSL)
- Grey70 (Light at 70% on HSL)
- Grey80 (Light at 80% on HSL)
- Grey90 (Light at 90% on HSL)
- Grey95 (Light at 95% on HSL)
